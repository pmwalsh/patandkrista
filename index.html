Where

Our event will take place at Jennette's Pier. The address is 7223 So. Virginia Dare Trail | Oceanfront, Milepost 16.5, Beach Road | Nags Head, North Carolina 27959. There is plenty of parking and it is free.

Weather permitting, the ceremony will occur on the beach to the left of the pier, followed by a cocktail hour and reception dinner inside. In the event of inclement weather, the ceremony will also be moved indoors.
